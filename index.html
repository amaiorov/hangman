Hangman

______ ! \| | | | | /| =========



How to play
  • Enter a word in the box, 5 characters or more
  • Hit Start button or the <enter> key on your keyboard
  • Type a letter a-z, lowercase
  • If your guess is correct, all occurences of the letter will be shown
  • If your guess is incorrect, the hangman will progress towards his demise
  • Once you guess the complete word, you win
  • If you fail to guess the complete word after 7 attempts, you lose
